Me in a Nutshell

Marketing

I have 5 years experience building integrated and innovative marketing programs for leading B2B brands across Finance, Technology and Professional Services.

I did this first at a boutique financial communications agency and later at The Wall Street Journal as Marketing Manager.

Business

At the agency, I was responsible for leading growing a rapidly expanding Marketing and Design unit within the larger business.

At the WSJ, I became proactively involved in sales and business development, often suggesting new revenue opportunities and providing industry education to enhance our value as a premium media partner.

Technology

Recently, I underwent a 12 week immersive coding program at General Assembly to better understand the fundamentals of technology which I believe is crucial to operating in today's world.

On top of becoming much more digitally astute, I've learned how to never give up and face my fears head on.

Worked with: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.JS, Ruby on Rails, AngularJS and Ionic

Globalized

Having lived and worked across three continents and travelled to well over 50 different countries, I love working with cross-cultural teams and learning more about the world.

Inspiration truly comes from anywhere, and it is my firm belief that the most fascinating ideas come from the most unassuming places.

Me in a Nutshell

Marketing

My first role was at a financial communications agency where I managed a portfolio of 10-12 global companies, advising on everything from brand storytelling and SEM strategy to event production and website development.

I later moved to The Wall Street Journal where I devised integrated marketing programs for a range of leading brands across the B2B space.

Business

As the main lead for the Marketing and Design unit within the agency, I was responsible for driving sales, managing team members and improving internal knowledge of the marketing industry.

Despite a more focused mandate at the WSJ, I proactively worked to promote new partnership opportunities, disseminate relevant industry insights and identify diverse revenue streams to enhance the commercial value of our business.

Technology

Recently, I underwent a 12 week intensive coding program to better understand the fundamentals of technology which I believe is crucial to operating in today's world.

On top of dramatically opening my eyes to the power of programming, I learned the value of maintaining unwavering perseverance in the face of seemingly insurmountable obstacles.

Global

Having lived in three different continents and travelled to well over 50 countries, my identity is strongly defined by my global experience. As such, the prospect of cross-cultural collaboration is one that I approach with the utmost humility and excitement.

Always one to ask questions and embrace a new learning opportunity, it is my firm belief that the most inspiring ideas come from the most unassuming places.
